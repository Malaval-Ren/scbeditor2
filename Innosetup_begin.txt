The application manages the outputs and inputs of a M221.
Outputs are able to remote control to be able to turn On / Off power of products in your cubicle.

And in parallel, the inputs are used if you are in front of your cubicle being able to turn On / Off power of products.

To help, the working from home, I have created a basic solution to be able to use my laptop to connect to my working PC desktop in my office.
Base on device Modicon M221CE family connected through port Ethernet.
